PROJECT OVERVIEW

Sowparnika Flamenco is a luxurious residential project by Sowparnika Projects located at Sarjapur Bangalore. The project offers Spacious Studio, 1bhk, 2bhk, and 3bhk Apartment ranges between 380 - 996 sq.ft with a perfect combination of contemporary architecture and features to provide comfortable living with a total of 4 Blocks S + 4 Floors, with a total number of 264 units.

Sowparnika Flamenco Project is Spread across 2.2 acres the project Location has excellent connectivity & it is located at the crux of Old Fort Road, Sarjapur near existing and upcoming communities & facilities.

Sowparnika Flamenco offers facilities such as Gymnasium, Lift, Landscaped Garden, Play Area, Rain Water Harvesting, Health Facilities, Car Parking, Jogging Track, 24Hr Backup Electricity, Security, and Sewage. It is an under-construction project.
